Guide 2026

NGOs that help

Non-profit organizations offering free legal and social support for the regularization process.

Main organizations

Spanish Red Cross

Social support, guidance, reports, food, clothing, referrals. Nationwide. Tel: 900 107 709.

Cáritas

Holistic support, financial aid, employment, housing and regularization programs. Diocesan offices in every province.

CEAR

Spanish Commission for Refugees. Specialized legal advice on immigration and asylum. Offices in 11 regions. Tel: 915 982 210.

Accem

Comprehensive care for refugees and migrants. Integration, employment, training and legal support.

Red Acoge

Federation of 18 pro-migrant NGOs. Local directory on their website.

SJM — Jesuit Migrant Service

Legal aid, defense of rights. Network: Pueblos Unidos, Migra Studium, Ellacuría and others.

Provivienda

Housing for vulnerable groups. Key for padrón registration and address proof.

SOS Racismo

Anti-racism information and complaint office. Advice on discrimination and administrative conflicts.

Acción contra el Hambre

Employment and entrepreneurship programs for migrants (Vives Emplea, Vives Emprende).

What they offer

  • Free legal advice specialized in immigration.
  • Companion services for appointments at Immigration, Social Services or Police.
  • Supporting reports complementing the municipal vulnerability certificate.
  • One-off financial aid, food, clothing and hygiene kits.
  • Spanish courses, employment training.
  • Help with padrón registration, opening a bank account, accessing healthcare.

⚠ Beware of paid "agents"

All listed NGOs are free. If anyone charges you to "arrange your regularization" or "guarantee your appointment", it is likely fraud. Report it to the National Police or the Ministry.
