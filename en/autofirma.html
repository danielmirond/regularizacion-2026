Autofirma

What is it?

Autofirma is the Spanish Government's free desktop app to digitally sign PDF, Word, XML or any file with your digital certificate. Open source, available on Windows, macOS, Linux and mobile.

Download

Download Autofirma (official)

How to sign a PDF

  1. Open Autofirma.
  2. Select the PDF file.
  3. Click 'Sign'.
  4. Choose your digital certificate.
  5. Enter password if required.
  6. Save the signed PDF.
