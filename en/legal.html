1. About this site

This website is an independent, non-profit citizen project aimed at providing practical information to migrants about Spain's extraordinary regularization 2026.

  • Nature: informational, unofficial website.
  • Purpose: collect and link information published on official government sources (Ministry of Inclusion, BOE, city councils, Mercurio) and recognised NGOs (CEAR, Red Cross, Cáritas, Accem).
  • Unofficial: this site does not handle procedures and does not replace the Ministry or any public administration.
  • Free: we charge nothing for any content or service, and we never will.

4. Privacy policy

4.1 What data we collect

None. This website does not collect personal data. No contact forms, no sign-up, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking of any kind.

4.2 Local storage

The browser's localStorage is used only to remember your preferred language. It never leaves your device. You can clear it from your browser's menu at any time.

4.3 Third-party services

The site loads technical resources from the following delivery networks:

  • Google Fonts, to serve the Inter, Fraunces and Noto typefaces. Google may log your IP when loading fonts. Google policy.
  • Tailwind CDN and unpkg, to serve technical JS/CSS. No cookies.
  • OpenStreetMap, for maps. OSM receives your IP when loading tiles. OSM policy.

You can use a blocker for these resources; the site will remain readable with system fonts and without the map.

4.4 Voice recognition

The voice assistant uses the browser's SpeechRecognition API. Your audio is processed locally or, in some browsers (Chrome), sent to Google servers for transcription. See your browser's policy.

5. Cookies

This site uses no cookies of any kind: neither first-party nor third-party, technical, analytical or advertising. Therefore no consent banner is shown (per Spanish Data Protection Agency, AEPD, cookie guidelines, Nov 2023).

The use of localStorage for language preference is technical and exempt, as would be a strictly necessary cookie.

6. User rights

As we do not collect personal data, no rights of access, rectification, deletion or objection apply (GDPR / LOPDGDD). If you find outdated or incorrect content, let us know and we'll fix it.
