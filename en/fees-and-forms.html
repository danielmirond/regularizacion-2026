Fee 790-052

The 790-052 fee is the mandatory administrative payment for residence and work permits in Spain. Paid through model 790-052.

Reference amounts

  • Temporary residence permit by exceptional circumstances: ~€10.72
  • Residence and work permit (employed): ~€211
  • Residence and work permit (self-employed): ~€211
  • Renewal / modification: ~€16

Approximate amounts. Official current figures are on the Ministry of Inclusion website.

How to pay

  1. Generate model 790-052 in the official portal.
  2. Fill in your data (NIE or passport) and select the procedure code.
  3. Pay online by card, bank debit, or print and go to a partner bank (CaixaBank, Santander, BBVA, Sabadell…).
  4. Keep the stamped payment receipt: it is mandatory in the file.

Form EX-32

The EX-32 is the official application form for temporary residence permit by exceptional circumstances. It's the form you must sign and submit for the extraordinary regularization.

  • Download the editable PDF from the Ministry website.
  • Fill in personal data, address, employment status and requested route.
  • Sign (handwritten or digital) and attach to the file with all documentation and the paid fee.

FAQ

Can I pay the 052 fee in cash?

Usually not. Must be via online banking, direct debit or bank counter with the printed model.

Is a simple ticket valid?

No. You need the receipt with the NRC (Complete Reference Number) or a bank stamp.

What if I make a mistake on the form?

Generate a new one. Generating 790 models is free; you only pay when actually submitting.

Can I sign EX-32 digitally?

Yes, if you file through Mercurio. In-person submissions use handwritten signature.
