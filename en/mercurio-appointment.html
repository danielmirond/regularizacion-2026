Mercurio · Appointment booking

Steps

  1. Go to icp.administracionelectronica.gob.es.
  2. Select province and procedure.
  3. Enter passport/NIE.
  4. Choose an available slot.
  5. Save the confirmation.

If no slots

  • Retry at different hours (early morning, late night).
  • Try different procedure types.
  • Weekends and holidays also release slots.
  • Ask NGOs for help.

On the day

  • Arrive 15 minutes early.
  • Bring confirmation printed or on mobile.
  • Passport original + copy.
  • All supporting documents.
