Guide 2026

Criminal Record Certificate

How to obtain it in Spain and in your country of origin. Hague apostille, sworn translation, online applications and timelines.

Criminal record in Spain

  • Online with digital certificate, DNIe or Cl@ve.
  • Fee ~ €3.78.
  • Instant (online) or 1–3 days (in person).
  • Validity: 3 months.
Apply (Ministry of Justice)

Criminal record from your country of origin

Required for every country you have lived in during the past 5 years, with:

  1. Hague apostille (or consular legalization if your country is not a party).
  2. Sworn translation into Spanish if in another language.

Guide by country

🇻🇪 Venezuela

Ministry of the Interior. Online at mpprijp.gob.ve. Apostille by Foreign Ministry. Total time: 4–12 weeks.

National Police. Online at antecedentes.policia.gov.co. Apostille at Cancillería. 1–3 weeks.

Judicial Power + Public Ministry. Online at pj.gob.pe. Apostille by Foreign Ministry. 2–4 weeks.

🇦🇷 Argentina

Registro de Reincidencia. argentina.gob.ar/justicia. 1–3 weeks.

🇪🇨 Ecuador

Ministry of Interior / National Police. Online with cédula. 2–4 weeks.

REJAP. Apostille at Foreign Ministry. 3–6 weeks.

🇲🇦 Morocco

Ministère de la Justice. Consular legalization required. 2–6 weeks.

🇸🇳 Senegal

Ministère de la Justice. Consular legalization. 4–8 weeks.

Hague apostille

  • Applied by the issuing country, not Spain.
  • If your country is not a party to the 1961 Hague Convention, you need consular legalization instead.
  • Documents not in Spanish require sworn translation.

Sworn translation

Done by a sworn translator appointed by Spain's Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MAEC). Official list:

Sworn translators, MAEC

Approximate cost: €30–€80 per document.

FAQ

Do I need the Spanish criminal record if I don't have NIE yet?

Yes. The Ministry of Justice issues it using your passport.

What if I lived in several countries?

You need one certificate per country lived in during the last 5 years, all apostilled/legalized and translated.

Does it expire?

Yes, 3 months from the issue date.

Total cost?

€50–€200 per country, including fee + apostille + sworn translation + shipping.
