Mercurio — Mass Regularization

What does 'mass regularization' mean?

It's the colloquial term for the 2026 Extraordinary Regularization. Official documents call it 'temporary residence permit by exceptional circumstances'.

Procedure in Mercurio

  1. Get a digital certificate or Cl@ve.
  2. Gather documents (passport, padrón, criminal record apostilled, vulnerability certificate, proof of rooting, job offer).
  3. Pay fee 790-052.
  4. Complete and digitally sign the EX-32.
  5. Log into Mercurio → 'Initial application'.
  6. Upload PDFs and submit.
  7. Monitor DEHÚ for notifications.

Routes available

  • Social arraigo: 2 years in Spain + social report + integration proof.
  • Social-family arraigo.
  • Labour arraigo: 6 months proven activity.
  • Training arraigo.
